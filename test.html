The SETA's AUDIT requirements include:

  • LEARNER ATTENDANCE

    • Supervisors are required to manage the Learner’s attendance at the workplace. A Daily Attendance register must be maintained and signed off by the Learner and Supervisor. Supervisors must APPROVE all the Learner’s Leave. If the Learner stays away from work without permission, such leave should be marked as UNPAID Leave.

  • STIPEND CLAIM

    •  The Supervisor’s must validate the Monthly Stipend Claim submitted by the Learner.

  • PERFORMANCE REPORTS

    • The Supervisors must submit regular Performance Reports on the performance, achievements, attributes and attitude of the Learner.
